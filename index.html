Initiatives Driving Real Change

We channel efforts into targeted domains to guarantee sustainable, positive outcomes for communities and ecosystems.

Education Initiative

Project Bachpanshala

About Bachpanshala

Project Bachpanshala focuses on taking quality primary education directly to children who lack access. We build small community classrooms to teach foundational mathematics, languages, and moral sciences.

Key Areas & Milestones

  • Digital Classrooms: Equipping basic tablets and computers to teach basic computer usage and digital navigation.
  • Learning Kits: Distributing textbooks, notebooks, drawing supplies, and learning materials.
  • Extra-curricular Mentorship: Instilling life skills, personal hygiene awareness, and creative thinking through games and interactive sessions.

We aim to bridge the gap so these kids can transition seamlessly into standardized public or private schooling environments.

Humanitarian Assistance

Project Seva

About Project Seva

Project Seva operates as InAmigos Foundation's direct aid response mechanism, acting as a lifeline for families undergoing intense economic challenges.

Core Actions

  • Food Distribution: Conducting weekend food drives in slums and around railway stations, distributing fresh hot meals.
  • Winter Relief Drives: Distributing high-quality blankets and warm woolens to people sleeping rough on roadsides.
  • Ration Supplies: Delivering monthly kitchen staples (grains, pulses, oil, spices) to marginalized households and old age homes.

Through Project Seva, we fight daily hunger and strive to ensure that basic physical needs of survival are met with dignity.

Women Empowerment

Project Udaan

About Project Udaan

Project Udaan stands for freedom, health, and economic sovereignty. We work extensively in rural sectors to break taboos regarding women's wellness and build livelihood opportunities.

Focus Verticals

  • Menstrual Hygiene Campaigns: Dispel cultural myths surrounding menstruation and distribute biodegradable sanitary napkins to rural girls.
  • Self-Help Group (SHG) Partnerships: Training women in handicrafts, sewing, and small-scale manufacturing, helping them market their products.
  • Financial Literacy: Teaching basic accounting, opening bank accounts, and highlighting micro-savings benefits to foster independence.
Environmental Conservation

Project Prakriti

About Project Prakriti

Project Prakriti acts as our environmental restoration shield, mobilizing students and citizens to counter rising urban concrete landscapes and preserve soil vitality.

Milestones & Workareas

  • Aforestation Campaigns: Planted 20,000+ native saplings across parks, highway reserves, and local school premises.
  • Cleanliness Drives: Cleaning water bodies, lakes, and public spaces, discouraging single-use plastic.
  • Eco-Agriculture Promotors: Educating small farmers in composting, rain-water harvesting, and reducing chemical fertilizer dependencies.
Animal Protection

Project Jeev

About Project Jeev

Project Jeev operates with the ethos that humanity's circle of kindness must encompass all living beings. We look after stray dogs, cats, and birds living in difficult street environments.

Initiatives

  • Stray Feeding Network: Volunteers prepare and distribute nutritious food to stray dogs daily.
  • Water Bowl Project: Setting up clay water bowls across neighborhoods during hot Indian summers to prevent dehydration in animals.
  • Medical Coordination: Directing rescues, tying reflective collar bands to prevent road accidents, and facilitating medical support.
Youth Empowerment

Project Vikas

About Project Vikas

Project Vikas is a massive training ecosystem that helps students transition into corporate, technical, and creative careers by providing internships and industry-focused training.

Corporate Skill Training Modules

  • In-demand Skills: Technical writing, financial research, content management, digital marketing, and HR methodologies.
  • Practical Workshops: Conducting webinars on resume formulation, corporate communications, and interview readiness.
  • Internship Schemes: Facilitated over 30,000 student internships, providing them hands-on experience, guidance, and validation certificates.

About InAmigos

A Vision of Equal Opportunity & Environmental Health

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, the InAmigos Foundation has grown from a localized youth collective to a nationwide network of volunteers and professionals.

We work tirelessly to support India's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), focusing on zero hunger, quality education, clean environments, gender equality, and decent economic opportunities.
